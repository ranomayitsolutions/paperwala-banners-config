Privacy Policy

App Name: Paper wala

Last Updated: 16 February 2026

Paper wala ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Paper wala, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register using Email or Google Sign-In, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if provided by Google)

Authentication is handled securely using Firebase Authentication.

1.2 User-Generated Content

  • Saved paper drafts
  • Generated question papers
  • Quiz/test attempts
  • Chapter selections

This data is securely stored using Firebase services.

1.3 AI Processing

When you use AI features, uploaded content (text or images) may be processed by third-party AI providers to generate questions. We do not sell or publicly distribute your content.

1.4 In-App Purchases

Premium features may be offered through in-app purchases processed by Google Play. We do not store your payment card details. All payment information is handled securely by Google Play.

1.5 Advertising

The app may display ads from third-party advertising networks. These networks may collect device identifiers, approximate location, and usage data to show relevant ads.

1.6 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • App usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To save and sync drafts
  • To generate AI-based question papers
  • To enable quiz practice
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To display advertisements
  • To improve app performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Firebase (Google services)
  • Google Play Billing
  • Advertising partners
  • AI service providers
  • Authorities when legally required

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Paper wala is designed for teachers and students. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental or institutional consent.
